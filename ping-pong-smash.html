Playable Game

Ping-Pong Smash

A browser port of my local two-player Pong variant with a timed smash mechanic, fast rallies, countdown serves, and deuce rules.

Back to Arcade Games

Play

Keyboard only · local two-player

Controls

Left Player

W / S move

D smash

Right Player

↑ / ↓ move

← smash

Match Rules

  • First to 7 points wins, but you need a 2-point lead.
  • If both players reach 6 and tie, the game enters deuce.
  • The ball restarts from the center after every point.

Extra Keys

  • P pause
  • R reset

The smash only works if the ball is approaching your paddle and you hit the timing window correctly, just like in the original Python version.
