Playable Prototype

Space Duel

A browser port of my two-player arena shooter prototype with item pickups, stun, level-based bullet spread, and quick local-versus tension.

Back to Arcade Games

Play

Keyboard only · local two-player

Controls

Player 1

WASD move

Space fire

Player 2

↑←↓→ move

Enter fire

Pickups

  • Green = heal
  • Gold = attack boost
  • Blue = speed boost
  • Red = stun your opponent
  • Purple = level up and add more bullets
  • White = radial nova burst

Extra Keys

  • R restart

For the web version, I remapped Player 2 to arrow keys and Enter so the game works better on normal keyboards and laptops.
