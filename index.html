ABOUT

I like building compact systems that feel thoughtful.

I’m especially interested in projects where design and logic meet: interfaces that feel playful, mechanics that create clear feedback, and learning experiences that become more engaging through interaction.

Even when a project is small, I want it to feel complete. That usually means I handle the idea, structure, UI, rules, and implementation myself.

STRENGTHS

Game-like thinking

I naturally think in loops, feedback, tension, pacing, and reward, even in non-game interfaces.

Solo execution

I am comfortable taking a small project from concept to a functioning, usable result on my own.
